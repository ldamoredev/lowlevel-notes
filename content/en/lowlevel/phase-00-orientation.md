# Phase 00 · Orientation

This atlas is a map from "I can program" to "I understand the machine." It's
organized as phases that descend from the mental model of the hardware, down through
C and pointers to assembly and the toolchain, then back up into systems and
performance — with an operating system built from scratch as the spine project, and
craftsmanship habits running through all of it.
